About us

Welcome to SalvageReseller.com, the premier destination for acquiring vehicles Copart auto auctions. Our mission is to provide a seamless, secure, and efficient platform that connects buyers with a vast selection of salvage and clean title vehicles at competitive prices. With over 19 years of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on being Copart's longest running broker and offering unparalleled customer service, expert guidance, and an unrivaled inventory that meets the diverse needs of our clientele.

Our Story

Inloher Corp which is SalvageReseller.com parent business was founded in 2004 by a passionate automotive enthusiast who recognized the potential for growth and opportunity in the salvage vehicle market. The founder saw a need for a platform that would allow buyers to access a comprehensive inventory of salvage and clean title vehicles without having to have a dealer license, and thus, SalvageReseller.com was born.

Over the years, we have evolved and expanded our services, becoming a trusted and respected name in the industry. Our commitment to excellence has allowed us to establish a lasting partnership with Copart Auto Auctions, enabling us to provide an unmatched selection of vehicles for our clients.

Why Choose SalvageReseller.com?

Extensive Inventory: We offer access to an extensive and constantly updated inventory of more than 150k vehicles from Copart Auto Auctions. Our selection includes cars, trucks, motorcycles, boats, ATVs, RVs, and more, ensuring that you can find the perfect vehicle to suit your needs.

Competitive Pricing: By sourcing our inventory directly, we are able to offer our clients competitive pricing on a wide variety of vehicles. This allows you to acquire the vehicle you desire at a fraction of the retail price.

Expert Guidance: Our team of experienced professionals is always available to assist you throughout the entire buying process. From providing detailed information on specific vehicles to helping you navigate the bidding and purchasing process, our team is dedicated to ensuring your satisfaction.

Secure and Transparent Transactions: At SalvageReseller.com, we value the trust our clients place in us. That's why we are committed to providing a secure and transparent platform for conducting transactions. We protect your personal information and make sure that you are always aware of the status of your purchase.

Nationwide Shipping: We understand the importance of convenience, which is why we work with a shipping broker that offers nationwide shipping options to ensure that your newly acquired vehicle reaches your desired location safely and promptly.

User-Friendly Platform: Our website has been designed to provide a user-friendly experience, allowing you to search, bid, and purchase vehicles with ease. With advanced search filters and detailed vehicle information, you can find the perfect vehicle in just a few clicks.
